## 4. Findings & follow-ups

1. **RPO window acceptable but should shrink to <1h**: PITR baseline deployed (RPO ~5 minutes in steady-state). For drill we relied on a 6-hour-old logical backup — acceptable for catastrophic region loss, but PITR is the production target.
   - **TODO:** Enable PITR on a multi-region replica; track via `supabase_pitr_window_hours` metric.
2. **Slow first-byte on cold S3 object (1.4 GB)**: ~3 minutes to begin streaming.
   - **TODO:** Pre-warm by issuing `cp` on the object during the 24h preceding a scheduled drill (not strictly needed but reduces drill RTO).
3. **`oss2` not yet in production dependencies**: not exercised in this drill since the test bucket was S3.
   - **TODO:** Add `oss2` to requirements.txt before any China-region restoration.

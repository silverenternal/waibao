### 1. Scope of Pilot

Provider grants Customer a **non-exclusive, non-transferable** right to use the waibao platform (the "Service") for a **30-day evaluation period** (the "Pilot"), starting on the Effective Date.

### 2. Pilot Entitlements

| Item | Quota |
|---|---|
| Talent partner seats | 5 (extendable to 20 on request) |
| Candidates processed | 500 / month |
| AI interview sessions | 50 / month |
| Storage | 50 GB |
| Support | Email, 24h response, CSM 1v1 onboarding |
| Data residency | UK / EU / CN / SG (Customer chooses) |

### 3. Customer Obligations

- Designate a **single point of contact** (SPOC) for the Pilot.
- Provide feedback via weekly check-in (30 min) and a final NPS survey at Day 28.
- Use the Service in good faith; no reverse engineering, no reselling.
- Invite at least 3 internal users to create realistic load.

### 4. Provider Obligations

- Provision account within **24 hours** of Effective Date.
- Provide onboarding session within **48 hours** of provisioning.
- Weekly NPS pulse and a final report at Day 28.
- Service uptime target: **99.5%** during Pilot (credit if breached per SLA).

### 5. Data Protection

- Both parties comply with **GDPR** (EU/UK) and **PIPL** (China, if applicable).
- Provider is the **Data Processor**; Customer is the **Data Controller**.
- Data Processing Addendum (DPA) is incorporated by reference.
- Customer may export all data within **30 days** post-Pilot; Provider deletes thereafter.

### 6. Confidentiality

Mutual NDA covers all Pilot discussions, metrics, feedback, and roadmap items for **2 years** post-disclosure.

### 7. Intellectual Property

- Provider retains all IP in the Service.
- Customer retains all rights to their data and feedback.
- Provider may use **aggregated, anonymized** metrics for case studies (no PII).

### 8. Fees

**Pilot is free of charge.** No credit card required.

If Customer converts to a paid plan within 60 days post-Pilot, Provider grants a **30% discount** on the first 3 months of any Pro or Enterprise tier.

### 9. Termination

- Either party may terminate with **7 days' written notice**.
- Provider disables access; Customer exports data within 30 days.
- No penalty, no further obligation.

### 10. Disclaimers

The Service is provided **"AS IS"** during Pilot. Provider disclaims all warranties except as expressly stated. Aggregate liability is capped at **GBP 1,000**.

## 附录 A: Acceptance Criteria (Go / No-Go)

| 指标 | 阈值 | 计算 |
|---|---|---|
| NPS | ≥ 40 | `(promoters - detractors) / responses * 100` |
| 周活 (WAU) | ≥ 60% | weekly active users / accepted invites |
| 关键功能日活 | ≥ 50% | users using matching / interview / collab |
| 痛点 (bug + feature request 聚合) | ≤ 5 | top 5 by frequency |

如果 4 项中满足 ≥ 3 项 → Go (转付费)
如果 4 项中满足 1-2 项 → Pivot (延长 30 天)
如果 4 项中满足 0 项 → No-Go (停用)
